# Candidates

Mods and resource packs that are not currently part of the active pack but may need evaluation, testing, or a replacement decision during the Minecraft 1.21.1 migration.

## Paused and replacement candidates

| Project | What it does | Status and notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Better Statistics Screen](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-stats) | Overhauls the statistics screen. | Paused because it does not support DeathLog; see [the compatibility issue](https://github.com/TheCSMods/mc-better-stats/issues/108). |

| [ElytraPitch](https://modrinth.com/mod/elytrapitch) | Adds a simple elytra pitch display. | Simpler alternative to [FlightAssistant](https://modrinth.com/mod/flightassistant). |
| [EMI](https://modrinth.com/mod/emi) | Provides item and recipe browsing. | Evaluate as an alternative or companion to [REI](https://modrinth.com/mod/rei). |

Also compare [ResolutionControl++](https://modrinth.com/mod/resolution-control-plus-plus) and [ResolutionControl 3](https://modrinth.com/mod/resolutioncontrol3). |
| [Roughly Enough Resources](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/roughly-enough-resources) | Adds world-generation information to REI. | Previously listed as an optional [REI](https://modrinth.com/mod/rei) extra. |
| [StepUpAgain](https://modrinth.com/mod/stepupagain) | Steps up full blocks without jumping. | Possible maintained replacement for [StepUp Next](https://modrinth.com/mod/stepup). |
